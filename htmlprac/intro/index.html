From Bihar to Meerut: The Educational Journey of Aditya Kumar

Introduction..
My name is Aditya Kumar, and I am a resident of Khadaha Tola, a village in the state of Bihar. This is a brief account of my journey, driven by a dedication to education and a desire to learn, which has taken me from my home state to the city of Meerut in Uttar Pradesh.

Schooling and Skill Development


I began my academic journey in my home state. I successfully passed my 10th-grade (Matriculation) examinations in 2022. Just two years later, in 2024, I completed my 12th-grade (Intermediate) studies. Alongside my traditional schooling, I focused on enhancing my technical abilities. I completed a 'Kushal Yuva Program' (KYP) course, which equipped me with fundamental computer literacy and essential soft skills. This experience solidified my growing interest in the field of technology.

Current Goal: Higher Education in Computer Applications

After finishing my 12th grade, I decided to pursue a career in technology. With this goal in mind, I enrolled at Shobhit University, Meerut. Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) degree. This course is providing me with an in-depth understanding of software development, programming, and computer science, building a strong foundation for a successful career in tech.
